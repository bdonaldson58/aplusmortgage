    Bio

I have been through so many tutorials, and I am currently going through a course on FreeCodeCamp.com, which is an amazing program. But I am also developing my own projects on the side, because that is how I learn best, and that's what makes coding so much fun. It is my dream to start a career as a software or web developer and I am doing as much as possible to make that dream a reality.
