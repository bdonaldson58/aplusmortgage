Bio

My name is Brendan Donaldson. I grew up in Saint Joseph, Missouri. I graduated from the University of Missouri with a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration, where I also was a 2 year letterwinner in Football.

When I first stumbled upon an HTML course on Khan Academy in early 2015, I didn't realize it at the time but I was finally figuring out what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. Ever since then, coding has been a hobby of mine, and I have been learning as much as possible. I have been through so many tutorials, and I am currently going through a course on FreeCodeCamp.com, which is an amazing program. But I am also developing my own projects on the side, because that is how I learn best, and that's what makes coding so much fun. It is my dream to start a career as a software or web developer and I am doing as much as possible to make that dream a reality.
