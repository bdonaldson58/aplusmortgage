Definitions

    A

Annual Percentage Rate (APR)

A measure of the cost to borrow money, expressed as a yearly percentage. For a mortgage, this can include interest rate plus other fees, including mortgage insurance and origination fees.

Application

A printed form used by a mortgage lender, to record necessary information for a potential mortgage.

Appraisal

A report made by a qualified person to determine the estimated worth of a property.

    C

Closing

The closing includes the delivery of the deed, the signing of the loan documents, and the movement of money required to finish the transaction.

Closing Costs

Money paid by the borrower at the time of closing. This includes an origination charge, discount points, and other fees.

Conventional Mortgage

A mortgage that is not under a government program (such as FHA or VA).
